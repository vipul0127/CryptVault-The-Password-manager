Welcome to CryptVault

CryptVault is an offline password manager built with Django that allows you to securely store, manage, and retrieve your passwords—completely on your device, with no cloud dependency.

Key Features

Encrypted Storage

Stores passwords securely with user-selectable encryption types, protecting your data even if the local database is accessed.

Offline-First

Operates completely offline—no data is sent to the cloud, making it ideal for privacy-conscious users.

Secure Master Password

Protects your vault with a master password required to decrypt stored credentials.

Multiple Encryption Options

Choose your preferred encryption type, such as AES or RSA, for enhanced security.

Web Interface

A simple Django-based UI to add, edit, search, and retrieve passwords with user authentication.

Custom Categories

Organize passwords into categories like work, personal, bank, or social media.

About CryptVault

CryptVault is built for those who prioritize security and privacy. With a focus on offline functionality and strong encryption, it ensures your sensitive data stays safe on your device. Whether you're a privacy enthusiast, a developer, or just someone looking for a secure way to manage passwords, CryptVault is designed for you.

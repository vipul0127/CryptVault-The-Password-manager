Privacy Policy

At CryptVault, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we protect your information when using our password management application and its browser extension. Built on principles of trust and security, we adhere to stringent data protection standards.

1. Information We Collect

CryptVault collects only the credentials (usernames, passwords, and associated URLs) you choose to store in the application. These are encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored locally in a SQLite database on your device. The browser extension communicates with the application’s local server (http://127.0.0.1:8000) to retrieve credentials. No personal data is transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for storing, retrieving, and autofilling credentials within the CryptVault application and its browser extension. We do not collect analytics, track usage, or share your data with third parties, ensuring all operations remain local to your device.

3. Data Security Measures

We employ AES-256 encryption to secure your credentials in the SQLite database. The application requires a master password for access, which is cached securely during sessions. The browser extension operates under Chrome Manifest V3 guidelines, using secure communication with the local server. If your master password is lost, recovery is not possible to maintain security.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data. You can delete all stored credentials by removing the SQLite database file or uninstalling the application. For inquiries or data access requests, contact our support team at support@cryptvault.vipul-iiitd.in. We will respond within 48 hours.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally only as long as you use the CryptVault application. Uninstalling the application or deleting the database file permanently removes all data from your device, with no backups stored elsewhere.

6. Third-Party Services

CryptVault does not use third-party services for data processing. Any future features requiring external services (e.g., optional cloud backup) will include clear disclosures and require your explicit consent.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our application is not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If such data is detected, it will be deleted immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the CryptVault application evolves. Significant changes will be communicated through the application’s interface, with the updated policy effective from the date noted above. We regularly review our practices to comply with global privacy standards.

For further details or to exercise your rights, contact us at support@cryptvault.vipul-iiitd.in.
